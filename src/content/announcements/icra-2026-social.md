The co-chairs of the IEEE RAS Technical Committees on Robot Learning, Humanoid Robotics, and Robotic Hands, Grasping and Manipulation invite TC members to an informal social gathering at ICRA 2026.

This will be a casual evening to connect with colleagues, meet members of the broader robotics community, and unwind after the conference sessions over cocktails and finger food.

## Event Details

- Venue: TOGA Bar & Kitchen, Franz-Josefs-Kai 11, 1010 Vienna, Austria
- Date: Thursday, June 4, 2026
- Time: 19:00-22:00 local time

Due to venue capacity, participation is limited to 150 attendees. Registration is limited to members of the three TCs listed above and will be handled on a first-come, first-served basis.

Not a TC member yet? You can [join the relevant IEEE RAS Technical Committees](https://lnkd.in/gjT2QwrE) for free and then register for the social.

Please [register for the social](https://lnkd.in/g95362Ch). Submitting the form does not guarantee participation. Confirmations will be sent after registrations are verified with TC memberships.

The announcement is also available on the [IEEE RAS TC on Robot Learning LinkedIn page](https://www.linkedin.com/company/ieee-ras-robotlearning/posts/?feedView=all).
